The Thesis

The End of Heroic GTM

Most startups run their go-to-market on spreadsheets, manual outreach, and heroic effort. I build infrastructure that lets AI agents handle the entire revenue operations workflow.

For thirteen years, I operated inside hyper-growth tech companies — DigitalOcean, KeepTruckin, Cloudways — watching the same pattern repeat. Sales teams drowning in manual tasks. Marketing spending on leads that never get touched. RevOps buried in spreadsheets instead of strategy.

The breakthrough wasn't better CRM training or hiring more SDRs. It was infrastructure. AI agents that can browse job boards, enrich leads, write personalized pitches, schedule meetings, and follow up — all integrated into existing workflows.

"Not demos. Production systems that run 24/7."

— The Standard

This isn't about replacing humans. It's about removing the repetitive work that burns out talent and hides poor process. When AI handles the mechanical, your team focuses on the strategic — relationship building, creative campaigns, product feedback.
